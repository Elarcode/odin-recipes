Keto Yakimeshi

This is Grandma's recipe for the best version of a Yakimeshi there is!

Our family's restaurant has a fried rice dish called Yakimeshi Especial,

but my mom has figured out a keto version of this that tastes better than the original, somehow.


This recipe will also include how to make Tampico mix, which is added on the meal.

Ingredients for a family size meal:

  • 2 Big Cauliflowers
  • 5 Carrots
  • 1 Big Zuccini
  • Beef - 500 gr
  • Bacon - 400 gr
  • Chicken Breast - 500 gr
  • Kikoman Soy Sauce
  • Philadephia Cheese
  • Surimi (6 Crab bars)
  • 1 Chile Serrano
  • Mayonaise

Yakimeshi Steps:

  1. Grate cauliflowers, dice up veggies into small squares, dice up all meat into bigger pieces than the veggies.
  2. Fry bacon in a pan or wok, then fry beef, then fry chicken. Remove bacon when it's cooked, use its fat to give beef and chicken extra flavor.
  3. Add diced up veggies and cover with lid, add cauliflower about 5 minutes afterwards.
  4. Add Kikoman Soy Sauce towards the end, quantity is a personal choice.
  5. Serve with Philadephia cheese and Tampico mix on top. Add soy sauce and chipotle dressing to your liking.

Tampico Steps:

  1. Blend chile serrano in blender.
  2. Blend Surimi bars into food processor.
  3. Mix both blends into bowl, add mayonaise to your liking.
