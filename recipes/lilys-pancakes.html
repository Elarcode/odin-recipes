Lily's Pancakes

This is Grandma's recipe for one of my daughter's favorite breakfasts!

This is a great healthy alternative to traditional pancakes, using oatmeal flour as the base and fruits as sweeteners.

Depending on what kind of veggies or fruits are being used, they might need to be cooked, boiled or steamed beforehand.

Ingredients:

  • Oatmeal Flour - 1 cup
  • 2 Eggs
  • Fruits / Veggies mix - 1 cup
  • 1 Mashed up Banana
  • Milk - 1/3 of a cup
  • Peanut Butter - 1 tbsp
  • Cinnamon
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Blend oats to make the oatmeal oats to make flour out of it.
  2. Blend in the eggs, fruit / veggie mix, mashed up banana, milk, peanut butter and cinnamon all together.
  3. Pour and fry small pancake portions into pan with butter, slow heat, cover with lid while frying to ensure they're well cooked.
  4. Flip after a minute or two, then fry for another minute or two.
  5. Let them cool down, let toddler devour them.
