Tortillas de Harina

This is Grandma's recipe the best flour tortillas I know!

This recipe uses Mexican brand "INCA" for the fat in the batter, testing this in other countries might become a challenge.

Ingredients:

  • Vegetable Fat - INCA - 250 gr
  • Wheat Flour - 1 kg
  • Water - 500 ml
  • Salt - 30 gr

Steps:

  1. Whisk vegetable fat in a bowl until it gets a creamy consistency. An electrical whisk is recommended, but this can also be done with bare hands.
  2. Add salt to fat.
  3. Add flour into mix, mix by hand until no fat is visible anywhere in the bowl.
  4. Slowly add 500 ml of water to mix while hand mixing the batter.
  5. Keep mixing by hand for a long while. Make sure all ingrediends are mixed completely and no clumps are left anywhere.
  6. Once it's all mixed well, make balls of dough and place in plate or separate bowl.
  7. Once done making balls of dough with the entire mix, cover with plastic foil and let it sit for at least 30 minutes.
  8. Spread flour on tray or clean table to roll the tortillas in. You need some sleight of hand magic to make sure they end up round and even.
  9. Cook on pan on low heat stove, each tortilla should be done quick.
